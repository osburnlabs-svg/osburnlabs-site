Shoot Your Way Out

Tarkov-flavored survivor-shooter for mobile.

A top-down rogue-lite that fuses Escape from Tarkov's military atmosphere with the wave-survival loop of Vampire Survivors. Drag to move, auto-fire at the nearest enemy, level up to unlock weapons and skills, and survive as long as you can. Built ground-up for mobile.

Dull

Seven days. Honest work. The hotel has other plans.

Dull is a first-person psychological survival-horror game. You're the winter caretaker of a snowbound hotel closed for the season — keep the furnace lit, do your rounds, answer the radio, and survive seven nights as the place turns quietly wrong around you. Stress is the real enemy.

About

OsburnLabs is a small US-based studio — two brothers building games and apps on the side, because we wanted to make the kind of things we'd actually want to play.

We care about making honest software that respects the people using it: no accounts to create, no nagging, just games you can pick up and play. Shoot Your Way Out is the first thing we're putting real weight behind, with more on the way.

It's a hobby operation for now — funded out of pocket, with whatever the games earn going straight back into making more of them. If that's the kind of thing you like to root for, stick around.
